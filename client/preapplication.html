Every year, we recognize that our hackers have a diverse set of goals coming into our event. This year we are introducing two tracks with focused resources to target those goals: Emerging Track and General Track. We have designed resources specifically for the audiences they would be most effective for but all resources are available to everyone.

Emerging: New to hackathons and unsure of how to start building a technology project? The Emerging Hacker Track is here to provide you with guidance into introductory technology and how to ace a hackathon. Part of this track is a mentorship matching program to help guide you and your team through your first hackathon project. We will be offering workshops in Introductory App Development, Web Development, Data Science, and Gaming & Graphics designed for you (but open for everyone at the event!) to learn the fundamentals and get you started on your dream hackathon project!

General: **Already know the drill and feel comfortable building a technology project? The General Hacker Track is the conventional hacker experience, giving you full creative control and offering supplemental resources as necessary.** This track is here to give you opportunities to innovate and connect with others; we have set up a speaker series to expose you to industry professionals and new technologies to help you take your project to the next level.
